About HotelPro

HotelPro is a cloud-based hotel management platform built to help hotel owners run their properties efficiently — from a single room guesthouse to a multi-property chain.

We built HotelPro because hotel owners deserve simple, affordable, and powerful tools to manage bookings, staff, guests, and daily operations — without needing expensive enterprise software or complicated setup.

What We Offer

  • Real-time room and booking management
  • Guest self-service tools (ordering, requests, digital key)
  • Staff and department management
  • Multi-language and multi-currency support
  • Flexible plans — from free trials to full source-code ownership
