Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Click "Start Free Trial" on our homepage, fill in your hotel details, and choose a plan. Your account is created instantly.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, our Basic plan lets you try HotelPro free for 7 days before committing to a paid plan.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime from your dashboard.

Do you offer refunds?

Since HotelPro activates instantly, we generally do not offer refunds after payment — except for verified technical issues. See our Refund Policy for details.

What payment methods are supported?

We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and other methods via our secure payment partner, Cashfree.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your data is encrypted and stored securely. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I manage multiple hotels?

Yes, depending on your plan. Pro and higher plans support multiple hotel properties under one account.
